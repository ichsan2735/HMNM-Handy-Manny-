How do I get started with learning hands-on skills?

Start with basic safety courses, then progress to beginner-friendly tutorials in your chosen category.

What tools do I need for the courses?

Each course has a specific tool list. Basic tools include:

  • Safety equipment (goggles, gloves)
  • Basic hand tools
  • Measuring instruments
Is certification required for electrical work?

Professional electrical work typically requires certification. Our courses provide foundational knowledge but check local regulations for certification requirements.

How is course progress tracked?

Progress is automatically tracked through:

  • Video completion status
  • Quiz results
  • Practice exercises
